# Alphanumeria Examples

This document demonstrates real-world applications of Alphanumeria.

---

## Basic Words

HELLO → H2LL4  
WORLD → W4RLD  
NUMIA → N5M31  
LANGUAGE → L1NG51G2  

---

## Identity (ONID Concept)

Names can be transformed into unique encoded identifiers.

Example:

FREEMAN → FR22M1N  
BERNARD → B2RN1RD  

This creates:
- recognisable identity
- alternative naming systems
- consistent encoding layer

---

## Domains

Traditional domains are scarce and expensive.

Alphanumeria creates alternatives:

numia.com → n5m31.com  
hello.com → h2ll4.com  

This allows:
- new naming possibilities
- lower cost acquisition
- recognisable variations

---

## Password Concepts

Alphanumeria can be used to enhance memorability:

Example:
helloSecure → h2ll4S2c5r2  

This maintains:
- human readability
- increased variation
- personal encoding patterns

---

## Messaging

Encoded phrases can be used in communication:

Example:
“hello world” → h2ll4 w4rld  

This creates:
- shared understanding
- light obfuscation
- cultural signalling

---

## Philosophy

Alphanumeria is not just a tool.

It is a system for:
- identity
- communication
- coordination
