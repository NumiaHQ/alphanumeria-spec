# Level 1 — Alphanumeria Base Mapping

Level 1 is the foundational layer of Alphanumeria.

It defines a deterministic mapping between vowels and numbers.

---

## Mapping

A = 1  
E = 2  
I = 3  
O = 4  
U = 5  
Y = 6  

---

## Rules

1. Replace vowels only
2. Consonants remain unchanged
3. Case does not affect output
4. Mapping is fixed and universal
5. No randomness or variation

---

## Examples

HELLO → H2LL4  
NUMIA → N5M31  
SYSTEM → S6ST2M  
VALUE → V1L52  
IDENTITY → 3D2NT3T6  

---

## Properties

- Human-readable
- Easy to learn
- Deterministic
- Consistent across all users

---

## Purpose

Level 1 exists to:
- onboard users into the language
- provide a shared reference system
- act as the base layer for future complexity

---

Level 1 is not the end.

It is the beginning.
